]]> Floki has formed a strategic marketing partnership with DEXTools to launch a campaign that will expose the Floki brand to 140 million+ crypto users.

DEXTools is the leading decentralized token data analytics platform worldwide, with over 140 million users annually from all over the world, and support for over 70 blockchains!

Floki branding will be strategically placed in DEXTools “Top Sponsor” and “Top Button” locations for 1 FULL YEAR, introducing Floki to 140 million users. This is a first of its kind deal and the very first time DEXTools will agree to a marketing partnership of this length.

The FLOKI-DEXTools campaign started on the 22nd of February, 2023 by 7pm UTC.

While our DEXTools campaign will introduce FLOKI to millions of people, the DEXTools audience is a super targeted, crypto savvy audience, which significantly increases the chances of converting them into holders or users of products within the Floki ecosystem!
